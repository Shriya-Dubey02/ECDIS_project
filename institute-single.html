AIMS INSTITUTE OF MARITIME STUDIES PVT LTD

Established in 2012, AIMS Institute of Maritime Studies has its registered office at Belapur, Navi Mumbai. Approved and assessed by Indian Registrar Quality Systems (IRQS), AIMS Institute of Maritime Studies stands 1st institute in India to be granted ISO 9001:2015 certification by IRQS. We have been providing international level maritime and nautical training since 2012. We believe in safety and focus on quality training. Our team of experts provide extensive training to meet the requirements of STCW, ISM CODE, ISPS CODE, safety training , SOLAS, MARPOL and MLC 2006. Our organization’s training centre is approved by Many Foreign Maritime Administration including MCA UK (Via Marlins UK), Panama Maritime Authority (PMA), Liberia Maritime Affair LISCR.
